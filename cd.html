Introduction

Vinyl records have stood the test of time, offering analog sound quality and a tactile listening experience. Whether you're a collector or just getting started, understanding vinyl's history and charm is the first step.

CD Care & Storage

Proper care ensures your records last for decades. Always handle vinyl by the edges, use anti-static brushes, and store them vertically in a cool, dry place away from sunlight.

CD Players & Equipment

A quality turntable and properly aligned cartridge are essential. Consider adding a preamp, good speakers, and vibration control for the best playback experience.
